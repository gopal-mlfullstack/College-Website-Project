About Our College

Bengal Institute of Science & Technology is dedicated to ensuring the best professional and technical education catering to the demands of the 21st century — since 2001. The college currently offers BCA(H) & BBA(H) courses, affiliated to Sidho-Kanho-Birsha University (SKBU), Purulia. We focus on academic excellence, skill development, and holistic personality growth through modern teaching methods and experienced faculty.
